Useful Information for Olim Hadashim

First Steps in Israel

Open a Bank Account:
Choose an Israeli bank and open a bank account. It is recommended to choose a bank that is near your home or work.

Ministry of Immigration & Absorption:
Once you've opened and activated your bank account, set an appointment with the Ministry of Immigration & Absorption (Misrad HaKlita) in your city. You will then start to receive your Sal Klita (basket of benefits).

Health Insurance:
Before you made Aliyah, you probably chose a Kupat Cholim (HMO). Go to a branch and get registered.

Ulpan:
One of your benefits is a Hebrew Language course (Ulpan). You will receive more information during your first visit at Misrad HaKlita.

School:
If you have children, set an appointment with the school manager in order to arrange schooling for them.
