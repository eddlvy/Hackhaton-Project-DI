First Steps in Israel

1. Open a Bank Account:
Choose an Israeli bank and open a bank account. It is recommended to choose a bank that is near your home or work.

3. Health Insurance:
Before you made Aliyah, you probably chose a Kupat Cholim (HMO). Go to a branch and get registered.

4. Ulpan: One of your benefits is a Hebrew Language course (Ulpan). You will receive more information during your first visit at Misrad HaKlita.

School: If you have children, set an appointment with the school manager in order to arrange schooling for them.
